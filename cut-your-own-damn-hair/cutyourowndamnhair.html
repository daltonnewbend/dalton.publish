cut your own damn hair

dalton was a graduating senior in their final semester when the COVID-19 lockdowns began. like many others, they were asked to continue their courses remotely, meeting online and contining to make work at home. dalton built an outdoor studio/exhibiton space where they set up the space each morning and broke it down each evening. the artist continued to work and make in this space. the work was exhibitied for the online version of drifting into the later, their senior thesis exhibition.
